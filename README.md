### Game Dev project 
The Casbah's Daughter is a story-driven 3D action-adventure game set in the heart of medieval
Algiers. Players take control of a brave young woman whose father is kidnapped by Spanish
pirates. Armed with wit, courage, and her fists, she must infiltrate a pirate ship, uncover dark
secrets, and confront a fearsome pirate captain to bring her father home.
The game celebrates the history, architecture, and culture of Algiers, immersing players in the
sights and sounds of the legendary Casbah.
